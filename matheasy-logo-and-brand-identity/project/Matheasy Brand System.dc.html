02
Construction · optical geometry

Drawn on a grid, corrected by eye

The mark lives on a 48-unit grid. A single stroke weight, round caps and joins throughout, and near-parallel ascenders keep it precise and reproducible at any scale.

6u
Stroke weight
A single constant weight for the whole gesture. No tapering.
≈53°
Ascenders
Both rising legs share one angle, so the checks read as siblings.
R
Round terminals
Round caps and joins throughout — friendly, never sharp.

A continuous down-up-down-up gesture, split only for the two tones.
The dark junction dot marks the single shared vertex where the receding check hands off to the leading check.
03
Symbol treatments

Two ways to render one mark

Two-tone is the primary, expressive treatment. Single-tone is the small-size and single-color workhorse. Same geometry, same stroke — no gradients, no effects, ever.

Two-tone

PRIMARY

The receding check drops to 42% weight of the leading tone. Use at 32px and above — logos, hero, marketing, the app icon.

Single emerald

MONO

One solid tone. Use at 24px and below, and for single-color print, embossing, engraving, and monochrome contexts.

10
Brand positioning & rationale

The mark is the promise

Matheasy turns math from something you fear into something you get. The identity carries that arc in a single gesture — you were here, and now you’re higher.

Understand

The first, receding check — the moment a concept clicks. We explain visually, step by step, until it makes sense.

Practice

The gesture between the checks — adaptive repetition that meets each student where they are and keeps them moving.

Master

The leading check — confident, higher, ahead. Mastery isn’t the end of the line; it’s the top of the climb.

Voice
Friendly Intelligent Trustworthy Modern

Encouraging, never condescending. Clear, never dumbed-down. We celebrate the click of understanding without the hype.

Audience

Students from primary school to university — and anyone who was ever told they “weren’t a math person”.

An AI-powered mathematics learning platform for understanding visually, practicing adaptively, and mastering with confidence.
